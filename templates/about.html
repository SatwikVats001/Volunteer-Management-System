About Our Volunteer Management System

This platform connects volunteers with available events. Volunteers can request to participate in events, and admins can approve or reject their requests. Upon approval, the volunteer is added to the event and assigned tasks.

For Admins

Admins can manage events, approve/reject volunteer requests, and assign tasks.

  • Manage Events: Admins can create new events and manage existing ones.
  • Approve/Reject Volunteer Requests: Admins will see the list of volunteer requests and can approve or reject them based on the volunteers' skills and availability.
  • Assign Tasks: Once the volunteer request is approved, volunteers are added to the event and assigned tasks based on the event's needs.

For Volunteers

Volunteers can browse available events, request to join, and be assigned tasks upon approval.

  • Browse Events: Volunteers can see all upcoming events and choose the ones they want to participate in.
  • Request to Join Events: Volunteers can send requests to join events they are interested in.
  • View Assigned Tasks: Once a request is approved, volunteers will be notified and added to the event with assigned tasks.
